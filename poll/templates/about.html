About the project

The IPL-2020 is coming near and will start on 19th sept 2020. As it begins, all the fans will start wishing their team all of our luck for their victory. To see which team has most popular fanbase. I have created a small Django project call IPL2020 poll. You can poll for your fav team and let's see how much support your fav team can get.

So start voting by clicking on vote button on top

The main purpose of this project was to learn how to learn how to use Django, to build a polling app. To built this we have used sqlite3 as a our database. For chart, we have used Chart.JS for visualization of result.
